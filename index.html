My Projects

PSYCHODELICA

A work in progress psychedelic 3D bullet hell shoot-em-up for desktop platforms. Players take control of a spaceship and have to dodge enemy fire, destroy enemy ships, and take down incredible bosses. This is a solo project with me assuming most development roles.

  • Created with Godot Engine
  • Heavy use of shaders written using GLSL

Curve Pong

A version of pong with additional mechanics and improved graphics, created for Ludum Dare 51. I worked on this game as part of a team of 4. Contributions were tracked through Git. My contributions include:

  • Implemented an Event Manager to support the script architecture
  • Implemented Post Processing Effects and additional VFX/Shaders
  • Designed and Implemented User Interfaces, including the Title Screen, Game Over Screen, and Scoreboard
  • Created additional Sound Effects for the game, such as the Explosion, Dash, and Curveball effects

Dialogue System

A JSON-based dialogue system created for an in-progress detective game. Supports multiple languages and accessibility settings. It features:

  • Branching dialogue paths
  • Multiple choice questions for the player
  • Player can question the speaker about any item in the game
  • Native BBCode support allowing for colored or animated text
  • Supports multiple languages, fonts (dyslexic, etc), and multiple font sizes

Reflection

A 2D sidescrolling platformer created in a team for a high school game design class at USC. My contributions include:

  • Programming most main game mechanics
  • Worked on collision system for levels
  • Implement artwork into game (level art and parallax backgrounds)

Various Custom Maps

Various custom maps created in the Hammer Editor for Counter Strike and Team Fortress 2. One was even featured on the front page of the Counter Strike workshop page.

  • Greyboxed and prototyped level layouts and designs
  • Playtesting and quick iterations

About Me

Heya~ I'm Sam Hansen (she/her). I'm a video game programmer/developer. I am primarily a programmer, but I work and have experience in most fields of game development. I love learning new things and love to take on challenging projects.

I am currently studying at UC Irvine for a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a specialization in Computer Networks. I am expected to graduate in Spring 2024.

I have been playing video games for most of my life. I am typically drawn to first person shooters, but I play a wide variety of games. Some of my current favorites are Citizen Sleeper, Valorant, ULTRAKILL, and Gloomwood.
